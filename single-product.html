Description

Product Overview

The High-Pressure Reverse Osmosis Membrane element is specifically engineered for industrial water purification systems requiring exceptional salt rejection and high throughput. With a focus on long-term performance and chemical resistance, this element provides consistent permeate quality even in challenging feed water conditions.

  • Active Surface Area: 400 ft² (37 m²)
  • High rejection rates for brackish water applications
  • Durable construction for multiple CIP (Clean-In-Place) cycles
  • Global standard for 8-inch pressure vessels
